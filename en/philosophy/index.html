MISSION
MISSION

When it comes to delivering luxury without ostentation and comfort without concessions, we are unrivalled. We bring a rational approach to every architectural and construction-related decision to optimize the experience of those who will enjoy the homes we build

We are obsessed with quality, because a person’s home is the bedrock of their wellbeing. If we pursue excellence, it is not just for the sake of saying it but because we truly believe there is no other option.

Constructing
a new standard of
excellence

The quality of our materials, the experience and engineering that contribute to our construction work, and our attention to detail mean that a square metre of a Caledonian home has a value that no other property developer on the market can offer. 

The constant search for perfection, as well as the satisfaction we obtain when solving architectural or engineering problems never before solved, or the level of demand and honesty when judging our work, allow us to speak of a new standard of excellence.

 

VISION
VISION

We maintain that excellence is an ongoing process of exploration, improvement and dedication to achieve quality in the final outcome. It requires a creative mindset, in-depth understanding of how the design of a home shapes the well-being of the inhabitants, and a sound understanding not only of the technology involved, but also of the opportunities it represents. 

Ever since we built our first residential development in Madrid, thanks to which we received the prestigious Leed Platinum certification for sustainable building, up to the point when we started to blur the boundaries between outside landscaping and interior spaces, our work has received various awards, a series of recognitions that have simply provided a further impetus for our unique vision of what a house should be. 

I would never sell
a house that I would not be
happy to live in myself

Enrique López Granados,

PRINCIPLES
WELL-BEING

At Caledonian we don’t limit ourselves to building houses. We build habitats. For this reason, well-being and comfort are the top priorities in every one of our decision-making processes

To achieve it, we adopt an integrated and rational approach where the engineering is used to construct true oases capable of nurturing our well-being.

EFFICIENCY

Efficiency is an attitude, and for this reason Caledonian homes are characterized by the judicious search for comfort.

By giving careful consideration to every design and construction decision, our homes offer greater energy efficiency and self-sufficiency.

QUALITY

Quality is non-negotiable. To do something well, it has to be done to the very highest standards.

As well as achieving pinpoint accuracy during construction and using only the best materials on the market, we rigorously check every component, however small it may be.

TECHNOLOGY

Technology is a powerful tool that can be used to shape human experience.

By integrating state-of-the-art systems and implementing pioneering construction practices, we improve the levels of comfort and functionality of every house.

AESTHETICS

Caledonian houses are characterised by their bright interiors and their welcoming minimalism – they have a modern yet timeless feel that offers a

blank canvas so that each new owner can identify with and feel at ease in their new space from the moment they move in.
