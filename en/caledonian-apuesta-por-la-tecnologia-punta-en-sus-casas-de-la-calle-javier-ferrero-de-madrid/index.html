Caledonian GOES FOR CUTTING-EDGE TECHNOLOGY IN ITS HOMES ON calle Javier Ferrero IN Madrid
Architecture
Sep 16, 2021
10 min
Going for technology when buying a property is an astute move. Technology is synonymous with quality, modernity and cutting-edge research applied to the production of goods that make life more agreeable and provide pleasurable experiences.

This idea has been brought to fruition in the development that CALEDONIAN is offering on Calle Javier Ferrero in Madrid, a luxury residential complex driven by the latest technologies.

 

Very few new apartments in Madrid are built today with such an advanced and sophisticated technological deployment. From the building’s conception through to the choice of materials, CALEDONIAN has always used the most technologically advanced systems and elements, to the point that the Javier Ferrero development is leading the way in property construction in Madrid.

    • CUTTING-EDGE TECHNOLOGY IN CONSTRUCTION

    To better understand the company’s commitment to technology, all you need to do is take a look at the methods used in the building’s construction.

    A GLASS FAÇADE

    Glass covers the entire frontage, from floor to ceiling, of one of the buildings. This is a real technological feat that entailed sourcing a type of glass that offers the best combination of resistance and insulation in terms of both heat and sound.

    A Passivhouse DESIGN FOR ENERGY SAVINGS

    The Passivhouse building standard calls for the optimization of available resources. For example, accurately calculating the exposure of façades and windows to the sun can reduce its impact and making savings on air-conditioning.

     

    NEW APARTMENTS IN MADRID EQUIPPED WITH STATE-OF-THE-ART TECHNOLOGY

    If the construction methods used stand out for their high-tech approach, this is taken even further in the apartments’ installations and systems. For the interiors, CALEDONIAN has deployed a whole array of technology from which several elements stand out:

    BIOMETRIC FINGERPRINT ACCESS CONTROL

    Home security is crucial, and in this respect current technology offers impenetrable systems. In the case of the Javier Ferrero development, it was decided to provide the apartments with Ekey biometric fingerprint access control. This is a fingertip scanning system that recognizes the user and allows them to enter the property. The software that controls it is smart and learns from use.

    CENTRALIZED HEATING

    The hot water and heating systems are centralized and use geothermal energy. A powerful heat pump that combines geothermal and solar power is responsible for generating the hot water that feeds these systems. The equipment that uses the geothermal power is able to accumulate heat based on the stable temperature of the earth. Backed up by photovoltaic solar panels, these installations allow the provision of hot water and central heating without having to resort constantly to polluting sources of energy.

    ADVANCED HOME AUTOMATION SYSTEMS

    Another of the strengths of this technological deployment is the home automation system that has been designed to control all the apartment’s main systems: automated lighting, opening of blinds, configuration of rooms and ambiences, etc. The three buildings of the Javier Ferrero development have been fitted with the sophisticated KNX Home Automation system. This communication standard is the leader in Europe and ensures that all the devices are interconnected without the distance between them proving to be an obstacle and guaranteeing that no mechanism is isolated.

    STATE-OF-THE-ART TECHNOLOGY meets THE WARMTH OF A HOME

    With its firm commitment to cutting-edge technology, CALEDONIAN has taken giant steps in the quality of construction of luxury homes, offering dwellings in which research and development clearly work to make people’s lives easier.
