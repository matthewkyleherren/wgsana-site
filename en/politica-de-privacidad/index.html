WHAT IS THE LEGITIMATION FOR PROCESSING YOUR DATA?

INTERESTED PARTIES LEGAL BASIS
POTENTIAL CLIENTS
  • Art. 6.1.b GDPR: application, at the request of the interested party, of pre-contractual measures (quotes, order forms, bids for services...).
  • Art. 6.1.a GDPR: consent of the interested party (to send the information requested, to respond to enquiries made...).

The data that we request is appropriate, relevant and strictly necessary and under no circumstances is it obligatory to provide it to us, but not providing it may affect the aim of the service or make it impossible to provide.

HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR DATA FOR?

Your data will be kept for the minimum time needed for the correct provision of the service offered and to handle responsibilities that may derive from it and any other legal requirement.

WHICH RECIPIENTS WILL YOUR DATA BE DISCLOSED TO?

CALEDONIAN PROYECTOS Y CONSTRUCCIONES SINGULARES S.A.U. will not disclosure your data to any third party, without you being expressly informed of it.

Additionally, we wish to inform you that specific data, in accordance with current legislation or the contractual relationship held with CALEDONIAN PROYECTOS Y CONSTRUCCIONES SINGULARES S.A.U. may be disclosed to:

  • Banks and financial institutions for the payment of the services contracted and/or products purchased.
  • Public administrations with authority in the sectors of activity of CALEDONIAN PROYECTOS Y CONSTRUCCIONES SINGULARES S.A.U. when established by current legislation.

WHAT ARE YOUR RIGHTS WHEN YOU PROVIDE US WITH YOUR DATA?

The data protection rights that the interested parties can exercise, where applicable, are:

  • The right to request access to the personal data relating to the interested party.
  • The right to rectification or elimination.
  • The right to opposition.
  • The right to request the limitation of processing.
  • The right to the portability of data.
  • The right to not be the subject of automated individual decisions.

The holders of the personal data obtained, can exercise their personal data protection rights by sending a written communication to the business address of CALEDONIAN PROYECTOS Y CONSTRUCCIONES SINGULARES S.A.U, or to the email address provided for that purpose, manuel.solano@caledonian.es.

Models, forms and more information is available about your rights on the website of the national control authority, the Spanish Data Protection Agency, henceforth, the AEPD, www.aepd.es

CAN I WITHDRAW CONSENT?

You have the option and the right to withdraw the consent for any specific purpose granted at the time, without this affecting the lawfulness of the processing based on the consent prior to its withdrawal.

WHERE CAN I MAKE A COMPLAINT IF I BELIEVE THAT MY DATA IS NOT BEING PROCESSED CORRECTLY?

If any interested party believes that their data is not being processed correctly CALEDONIAN PROYECTOS Y CONSTRUCCIONES SINGULARES S.A.U, or that the requests to exercise rights have not been handled satisfactorily, you can lodge a complaint with the corresponding data protection authority, which is the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD) in the national territory, www.aepd.es.

SECURITY AND UPDATING OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

With the aim of safeguarding the security of your personal data, we wish to inform you that all measures of a technical and organisational nature needed to guarantee the security of the personal data supplied have been taken. All of this is to prevent its alteration, loss and/or unauthorised processing or access, as required by legislation, however absolute security does not exist.

It is important that you always inform us whenever your personal data changes in order for us to be able to keep it up-to-date.

CONFIDENTIALITY

CALEDONIAN PROYECTOS Y CONSTRUCCIONES SINGULARES S.A.U informs you that your data will be processed with utmost diligence and confidentiality by all the staff involved in any of the phases of processing. We will not assign or disclose your data to any third party, except in the cases provided by law, or unless the interested party has expressly authorised us to do so.
