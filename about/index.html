MISSION

WG-SANA was founded on the conviction that everyone of us deserve to live a happy, fulfilling and safe life, regardless of any physical or mental handicaps.

WG-SANA provides different levels of medical care depending on residents needs, but more importantly provides a home like environment where everyone is treated with respect and care.

Life is too
short to not
love living it.

Throughout our different residences -- which are all actual apartments and houses, not hospital type residences -- we offer varying levels of support. In some we have trained nurses on site 24 hours a day, while in others our residents take on more responsibilities.

However, in all do that we do and in all of these facilities, them most fundamental aspect is the genuine care and love that is shared throughout our residents and staff. In many ways, we are family. Not a typical one, but then again, these days, what is one.
